The Roast Revolution

Abdul Rehman, better known as Ducky Bhai, started his journey in 2018 with reaction and roasting content. His fearless, witty commentary on social media trends, TikTokers, and influencers quickly went viral. By 2020, he had become a household name, famous for his "Ducky Bhai Roast" series that garnered millions of views within hours.

Beyond roasts, Ducky Bhai has expanded into vlogs, podcasts, and podcasts with international creators. His beef with YouTuber "Unicorn Rich" and later reconciliation made headlines. In 2024, he won the "Best Digital Creator" award at the Pakistan Media Awards. His second channel, Ducky Vlogs, has over 1.8 million subscribers.
